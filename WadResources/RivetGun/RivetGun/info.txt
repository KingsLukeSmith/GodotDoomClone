Name: Rivet Gun

Class: 6
Type: Projectile
Palette: Doom
Summon: RivetGun
Ammo Type: Rivets
Altfire: Yes
Powered Mode: No
Added States: No


Description:
A powerful rivet tool heavily modified for combat, offset by a low maximum ammo capacity, slight inaccuracy, and slower firing speed. 
The rivets have the ability to pierce through multiple enemies.

Primary fire features a sustained fire mode.
Secondary fire shoots a rivet that explodes and releases 5 rebounding rivets in several directions.
